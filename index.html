How?

Featherweight

Respondo stays out of your way. It enhances and helps formalize processes you are already using.

Zendesk-like

Respondo uses a ticketing approach for social media posts. You can filter tickets by status and search through them by author name or content.

Twitter, Disqus and more

Respondo currently supports automatically loading posts from Twitter and Disqus and manually via webhooks.

Why?

Why would you care?

  • Respondo is a new way of using social media networks.
  • Respondo empowers copmanies to fully utilize community feedback.
  • Current tooling is inadequate, hacky, time-consuming and counter-intuitive for emerging workflows.
  • Respondo intuitively combines best practices from the most popular tools on the market.
  • Respondo introduces new workflows that weren't supported.
