Respondo

Featherweight Zendesk for Twitter, Disqus and more

What is Respondo?

Respondo is a ticketing system for social media posts. In functionality it resembles Zendesk. You can classify, search and filter tickets. It's primary purpose is to ease and formalize the handling of social media posts for brand accounts.

Featherweight

Respondo stays out of your way. It enhances and formalizes processes you were already using.

Zendesk-like

Respondo uses a ticket approach to social media. You can filter tickets by status and search them by author name or content.

Twitter, Disqus and more

Respondo currently supports loading posts from Twitter, Disqus and manually via webhooks.
