How?

Featherweight

Respondo stays out of your way. It enhances and formalizes processes you were already using.

Zendesk-like

Respondo uses a ticket approach to social media. You can filter tickets by status and search them by author name or content.

Twitter, Disqus and more

Respondo currently supports loading posts from Twitter, Disqus and manually via webhooks.

Why?

Things that are different with Respondo.

  • Query posts from all providers.
  • Leave internal notes on posts.
  • View posts from all providers in the same view.
  • Manage people on your team.
  • Automatically add users to a team based on the email domain.
  • Manually add tickets.
  • No passwords, just OAuth.
